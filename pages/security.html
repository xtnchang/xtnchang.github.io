Problem

Many of IBM’s DB2 for z/OS customers struggled to understand the DB2 security workflow. Security is crucial in database management; without it, millions of users’ data could be at risk.

Solution

I worked cross-functionally with writers, visual designers, and subject matter experts to prototype and code an interactive diagram illustrating the DB2 security concepts and workflow. I helped drive effective storyboarding and iterative wireframing.
